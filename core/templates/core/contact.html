Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Job Shala

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar and choose whether you're a job seeker or employer. Follow the registration process and you'll be ready to start in minutes.

Is Job Shala free to use?

Yes! Job Shala is completely free for job seekers. Employers can post jobs and access basic features for free, with premium features available for enhanced recruitment needs.

How do I apply for jobs?

Browse available jobs, click on any position that interests you, and use the "Apply Now" button. You can upload your resume and cover letter directly through our platform.

How long does employer approval take?

Employer accounts are typically reviewed and approved within 1-2 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your account has been approved.

Can I track my job applications?

Absolutely! Once you're logged in, you can view all your applications and their current status in your dashboard under "My Applications."

What if I need technical support?

Our support team is available to help! You can reach us via email at support@jobshala.com or call us during business hours. We typically respond within 24 hours.
